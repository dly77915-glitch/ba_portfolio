Project Overview

From manual ordering to a structured digital service flow

The Problem

The store relied heavily on manual paper-based ordering, leading to frequent errors such as wrong sizes and missing toppings, operational chaos during peak hours, and time-consuming end-of-day reconciliation. Additionally, high commission fees (20-30%) from third-party apps limited profitability and prevented the store from capturing customer data for retention programs.

The Solution

Proposed and designed an "Order & Pick-up" mobile application prototype tailored for Tra Anh. The system features a comprehensive user flow from menu selection and detailed beverage customization to a seamless payment gateway, effectively resolving operational bottlenecks.

Deep Dive: Core Solutions

Three system improvements behind the prototype

Solution 01

Digitizing Complex Beverage Customization Flows

The Problem

Manual paper orders caused frequent errors regarding cup sizes, sugar/ice levels, and toppings.

The Solution

Designed a strict "Add to Cart" Use Case flow that enforces step-by-step customization with logical constraints, ensuring accurate orders are sent directly to the preparation queue.

Solution 02

Multi-Actor Order Status Synchronization

The Problem

A lack of communication between Customers, Cashiers, Baristas, and Shippers led to delayed service and overlooked orders.

The Solution

Engineered a sequence flow connecting three distinct actors. Order statuses sync in real-time from "Preparing" to "Waiting for Delivery/Pickup" to "Completed."

Solution 03

Customer Data & Loyalty Management

The Problem

Heavy reliance on third-party apps caused a loss of customer connection, hindering remarketing and loyalty programs.

The Solution

Structured a robust database architecture to capture customer profiles, order history, and promotional code logic, laying the foundation for personalized marketing and customer retention campaigns.
